Is the NI Route Right for You?

The Northern Ireland Route is highly effective in the right circumstances. Using Northern Ireland as a logistics hub for UK–EU cross-border commerce can offer significant advantages, but it’s not a one-size-fits-all solution. Our services are designed to help you navigate these complexities and determine if the Northern Ireland Route aligns with your operational needs and growth plans. Use the criteria below to assess whether it fits your business.

Typically Suitable

  • Sell physical goods
  • Ship meaningful volume to the EU and UK
  • Want to avoid establishing multiple entities
  • Can route inventory through Northern Ireland

Typically Not Suitable

  • Sell services or digital products
  • Ship small quantities across markets
  • Cannot operationally use Northern Ireland
  • Require cross-border corporate presence

Eligibility depends on product type, volume, and operational setup. A formal assessment is always recommended.
